Chicken Rice & Veggies

An everday body, soul, and mind building meal

Prepwork

  • Dice or slice your preffered veggies. We are using Mushrooms, Yellow onions, Red peppers
  • Dice or slice a whole chicken breast
  • start cooking at least 1 cup of preferred rice in a rice cooker, or pot
  • Season your chicken with salt & pepper
  • Into a bowl, mix 1 cup soy sauce, 1/8th cup oyster sauce, and seasme seed oil
  • Add a sprinke of chilli flakes, and 1 teaspoon of honey. mix well

Cooking steps

  1. Add a glug of a high-smoke point oil into a large pan over high eat
  2. Add your chicken into the hot pan
  3. stir your chicken around every minute or so, until all sides are cooked, and the internal temp reads 155c +
  4. remove the chicken from the pan, and add in your veggies
  5. Lower the heat to medium-high and cook the veggies until your preferred doneness
  6. add the chicken back into the pan, and add your sauce
  7. Stir all ingredients until everything is incorporated
  8. Serve over your Rice
