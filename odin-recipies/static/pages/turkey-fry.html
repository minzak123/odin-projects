Turkey-Egg Fry

Me great gret great great great grandpapay's thrice removed Dog
Went to turkey farm. This turkey farm also sold 1 dish between 10am-12pm everyday
It was a Turkey-Egg fry, using the freshest of ingredients.
The recipie for this fry, has been passed down unchanged to me, and my descendats

Prepwork

  • Dice a small bell pepper of your choice
  • slice up a few mushrooms of your choice
  • Season your ground turkey with salt, and pepper
    • Crack, and whisk 2 eggs into a bowl
  • Cut up a handful of spinach

Frying the cook

  1. Add a glug of extra virgin olive oil to a large pan over medium-high heat
  2. Add your seasonsed ground turkey into the pan, breaking it up into small chunks
  3. Keep the turkey moving as it frys until it's browned all the way through
  4. Add your eggs into the pan, scrambling them into the meat
  5. Once the eggs are cooked to your liking, remove the meat and eggs, and add your veg
  6. Once the veggies are cooked to your liking, add back in your eggs, and meat
  7. pour a glug of Soy sauce into the pan, mix it in well with everything
  8. Once everything is incorporated, add your spinach and cover the pan with a lid
  9. When the spinach is wilted (about 45 secs- 1 min) remove the lid, and serve
